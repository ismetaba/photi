# T13 Test Plan — Job runner crash recovery

## Acceptance Criteria
1. `JobRunner.recover()` finds every row with `status='running'`. For each:
   - Increment `attempts`.
   - If `attempts >= MAX_ATTEMPTS` → mark `failed` with `lastError='interrupted'`.
   - Otherwise → flip to `queued` with `lastError='interrupted'`.
2. `buildServer` calls `recover()` before any optional `runner.start()`.
3. `runOnce()` semantics for healthy jobs are unchanged.

## Test Scenarios (Vitest)
- **Recovers a `running` row to `queued`**: insert a job with `status='running'`, `attempts=0`. Call `runner.recover()`. Row flips to `queued`, `attempts=1`, `lastError='interrupted'`.
- **Marks `failed` after max attempts**: insert with `attempts=MAX_ATTEMPTS-1` and `status='running'`. Recover → `failed`.
- **Does not touch other statuses**: `queued`, `done`, `failed` rows are untouched.
- **runOnce after recover**: a recovered job becomes pickable on the next `runOnce()`.
- **buildServer integration**: spinning up `buildTestServer()` after manually inserting a `running` row leaves it `queued`.
